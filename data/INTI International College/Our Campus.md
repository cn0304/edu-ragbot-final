## INTI International College Penang

Located amidst the beautiful parkland setting of Bukit Jambul’s educational township, INTI International College Penang serves to promote Penang as a regional centre of excellence for quality education. The College holds true to its promise of delivering a vigorous holistic education by providing world-class programmes delivered by passionate teaching staff.
