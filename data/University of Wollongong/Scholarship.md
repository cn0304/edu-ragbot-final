# UOW MALAYSIA EXCELLENCE AWARD SCHOLARSHIP
The most prestigious scholarship offered at UOW Malaysia. It provides full tuition waiver and every year, only a few distinguished students are awarded with this scholarship.

- Pre-University & Foundation Studies Program
- Diploma Program
- Bachelor’s Degree Program

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|Criteria|   |Value of the Scholarship|
|SPM|9A+s & above|100% tuition fee waiver for the duration of program in UOW Malaysia|
|O-Level|9A*s & above|
|A-Level|≥ 19pts/AAAB|
|STPM|CGPA ≥ 3.75 (3 or 4 subjects)|
|UEC|Agg ≤ 8 (5 subjects)|
|SAM/AUSMAT|≥ ATAR 90|
|CPU|Agg ≥ 95%|
|IB|≥ 40|
|Foundation/Diploma|CGPA ≥ 3.90|

**Applicants are required to demonstrate outstanding leadership quality, self-motivation and positive attributes, on top of the above academic achievement**

**Notes**:

1. This scholarship is based on **ACTUAL** examination results only. Forecast or trial results will not be accepted.
2. To be eligible for the scholarship, applicants must be NEW students at UOW Malaysia in 2025 intakes and have paid the full enrolment fee.
3. The scholarship application deadline, along with the submission of all required documents, is set at two (2) weeks after the start of each semester on the following dates: **17 February, 28 April, 23 June, and 29 September 2025**.
4. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview within one month after the application closing date. All shortlisted candidates are required to attend an interview. Failure to do so, will result in disqualification of application. Only successful candidates will be notified of the outcome after the interview.
5. All fees paid are non-refundable, regardless of the outcome of the scholarship application. Successful recipients are required to maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.66 (or equivalent) throughout their duration of study in order to maintain their scholarship.

# UOW MALAYSIA GRADUTE RESEARCH SCHOLARSHIP
The UOW Malaysia Graduate Research scholarship is offered to suitable candidates who are enrolling in [postgraduate research programs](https://www.uow.edu.my/study-at-uow-malaysia/postgraduate-studies/) in UOW Malaysia.

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Criteria|Value of the Scholarship|
|1. Applicants shall possess a Bachelor’s degree with a minimum CGPA ≥ 3.00 or its equivalent.<br>2. Must meet all prevailing academic and language requirements.<br>3. Must be a full time postgraduate (by Research) student registered at UOW Malaysia.<br>4. Applicant must be recommended by the respective school responsible for the supervision and assessment of the candidate’s postgraduate studies.|Up to 100% tuition fee waiver for the minimum candidature period of the program.|

**Notes** :

1. The scholarship awarded to the recipient is subjected to the following conditions:
    - Submission of satisfactory progress reports at the end of every semester
    - Applicants who do not meet the conditions above could have the scholarship withdrawn after the issuance of a warning letter.
2. The duration of the scholarship is to follow the minimum duration of the program the candidate is enrolled in.
3. Recipients are to assist their school as teaching/research assistants during the duration of their study.
4. Publication of research in International Indexed Journals is required for the recipient:
    - Master’s degree program – 1 journal publication
    - PhD program – 3 journal publications
5. Recipients are required to sign an intellectual property agreement with UOW Malaysia.

# ALUMNI DISCOUNT
In recognition that UOW Malaysia is part of the University of Wollongong global network of institutions, tuition discounts are available for alumni of UOW Malaysia, alumni of UOW programs completed at another UOW campus or partner institution.

**ALUMNI DISCOUNT**

- 10% waiver of tuition fees for alumni to continue their studies to Bachelor’s Degree program after their Pre-University / Foundation / Diploma Programs or continue their Postgraduate Program after their Bachelor’s Degree.
- 10% waiver of tuition fees for Alumni’s children to study Pre-University / Foundation / Diploma / Bachelor’s Degree or Postgraduate programs.

# FAMILY DISCOUNT
Available for new student who is family member of current student.

**FAMILY DISCOUNT**

- Applicable for programs: Pre-University & Foundation Programs, Diploma Programs and Bachelor’s Degree Programs.
- 10% waiver on tuition fee for subsequent family member if the first family member has completed or is still pursuing a program of study at UOW Malaysia.

**Notes**:

1. The discount is applicable for the year of enrolment.
2. Successful recipients are required to maintain a minimum CGPA of 2.75 (or equivalent) throughout their duration of study in order to maintain the discount.
