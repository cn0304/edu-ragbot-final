### Application Procedure

Applying to MSU is a fairly straightforward process. Follow the steps below:

#### 1. Choosing Your Course

If you haven't applied for a course, the first step as an international student is to choose one. View our available programmes below:

- [Undergraduate Programmes](https://www.msu.edu.my/international-students/admissions#)
- [Postgraduate Programmes](https://www.msu.edu.my/international-students/admissions#)

#### 2. Fill in the Application Form & Attach Your Supporting Documents

Applications can be made through our [online application form](https://www.msu.edu.my/international-students/admissions#). Please submit your application at least 2 months before your intended intake date to avoid delays.

Attach the following documents to your online application:

- Certified copies of all academic qualifications (transcripts and certificates)
- Certified copy of TOEFL/IELTS results (if applicable)
- Certified copy of your passport (including blank pages), valid for at least 1 year from intake date
- Six (6) identical [passport-sized colour photos](https://educationmalaysia.gov.my/how-to-apply/passport-photo-guidelines.html/) (front-facing, non-smiling, white background)
- No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your embassy (for African/Sub-Saharan applicants)
- Pre-Arrival Medical Examination Report – [view panel clinics](https://educationmalaysia.gov.my/how-to-apply/medical-screening/registered-overseas-clinics.html/)
- Scholarship/study loan proof (or a financial guarantee letter if self-sponsored)
- English translations of all documents (where applicable)
