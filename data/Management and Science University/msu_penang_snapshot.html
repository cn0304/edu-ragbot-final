Penang

Located in the second largest city in Malaysia, MSU College Penang is the leading college in the northern region. For those who prefer the experience of a major city with major multinational companies, MSU College Penang would be an idyllic place to pursue world recognized qualifications.

MSU College Penang provides a multidisciplinary of programmes for school leavers seeking higher education opportunities after SPM, STPM, A-Levels, UEC, Matriculation, Polytechnics and any other equivalent qualifications. Those lacking in paper qualifications can also pursue their dreams and transform their lives through microcredentials.

As a collegiate institution of Management & Science University (MSU), MSU College Penang offers an array of programmes from Management & Science University (MSU), leading UK Universities and Professional Bodies. (ACCA, CAT,LCCI).

Apart from the full and part time mode of learning, MSU College Penang also offers Blended mode of learning which specifically caters to those in the working world.

Call us at 04-226 8281 for more information on the programmes offered and dates of intake.
