# Scholarship

### Full Scholarship - Medicine

-   This scholarship covers the **full tuition fee**.
-   To indicate interest, applicants must select the designated checkbox in the **online application system** when applying for the programme. _A separate application is not required._
-   Applicants must hold an **offer letter** from RUMC and **make the deposit within the two-weeks deadline from the issuance of the offer letter** to qualify for scholarship consideration. Scholarship applications will be accepted up to **15th April 2025**.
-   Applicant must not be bonded to or in receipt of any financial aid or rewards from other educational institutions or organisations.
-   Applicants will be assessed on both **academic and non-academic achievements**.
-   Shortlisted applicants will undergo an **online assessments and interview** in mid-May 2025.

_RUMC’s decision on awarding these scholarships will be final and scholarship awards for conditional offer recipients are subject to meeting the specified requirements based on their final results._ 

_Scholarship recipients must fulfil the role of ambassadors for RUMC, actively representing and upholding the institutions values, mission and reputation._

_The outcome of the scholarship will be announced by the **end of** **May 2025**._

_Information is correct at time of printing / publication. RUMC reserves the right to amend the information without prior notice._

#### Apply before the end of March 2025 to be qualified for the Undergraduate Medicine scholarship.

#### Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (PTPTN) / National Higher Education Fund (NHEF)

PTPTN is a special fund established under the auspices of the Ministry of Higher Education to offer educational loans to all eligible Malaysia students pursuing their tertiary studies at selected institutions of higher learning in the country,

This loan will enable students to fully or partially pay their fees and their subsistence for the duration of their studies.

The scope of the PTPTN education loan facility is open to students pursuing studies at the diploma, first degree, master, doctorate and professional course levels in IPTs established:

-   Universities and Universities Colleges
-   Institut Teknologi MARA
-   Private Higher Education

**Eligibility to Apply;**

1.  Citizen of Malaysia
2.  Not exceeding 45 years of age at date of application
3.  Pursuing a full-time degree at RUMC (RCSI & UCD Malaysia Campus)
4.  Must meet RUMC entry requirements
5.  The remaining period of study at the time of application should not be less than one (1) year
6.  Has no other sponsor(s)

_Note: The PTPTN loan only covers the 2 ½ years’ course of study at Penang, Malaysia_
