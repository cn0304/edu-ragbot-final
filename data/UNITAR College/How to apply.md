## How to Apply

Students who wish to apply to UNITAR may consult our education counsellors and apply online.

The Online Application is open throughout the year. Candidates may apply for the [programme](https://www.unitar.my/programmes/) of their choice by clicking on the Apply Now button. Please fill up the required information and press the Submit button. We will contact and guide you through the application process. 

If you possess other equivalent qualification(s) such as a [Foundation](https://www.unitar.my/programmes/undergraduate/foundation/) or [Diploma](https://www.unitar.my/programmes/undergraduate/diploma/) from another University/College/Institution/Polytechnic, you are required to submit the following documents:

-   A certified true copy of Certificate of Completion(s).
-   A certified true copy of Academic Transcript(s).
-   All relevant information and materials related to your previous studies from SPM onward.
