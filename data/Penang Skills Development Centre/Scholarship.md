# Financial Assistance
Discover FutureTech Academy’s range of financial aid options designed to make your education journey easier!

### Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (PTPTN)
**Eligible Programs: School of Engineering**

#### Important Information

-   Students will only receive funding from Semester 2 onwards
-   The closing dates for each cycle of PTPTN loan application are 30 April and 31 October
-   The total loan approved is based on PTPTN’s discretion
-   Students must obtain a minimum of 2.0 every semester to receive PTPTN loan for the following semester

#### Terms & Conditions

-   Applicant must fulfill the following requirements to qualify for the loan application
-   Must be a Malaysian citizen
-   Must not exceed 45 years of age on the date of application
-   Must not have any other financial sponsorships, scholarships or loans
-   Must fulfill the entry requirements of the FutureTech Academy Diploma in Engineering program
-   Must have more than 1 year of study left at the time of loan application submission
-   Must have a Skim Simpanan Pendidikan Nasional (SSPN) account
-   Must have CIMB Savings Account under the applicant’s name
-   Must obtain PTPTN pin number from Bank Simpanan Nasional (BSN)
-   Loan application and disbursement procedure
-   The loan application guidelines are provided by FutureTech Academy to receive PTPTN loan for the following semester
-   Students will be briefed on how to apply online at PTPTN website and how to submit the relevant supporting documents to FutureTech Academy
-   The loan is disbursed directly into the applicant’s CIMB Savings Account (Individual)

#### Terms & Conditions

-   Repayment/Financing of the education loan will begin 6 months after students obtain the last payment from PTPTN

### PTPK (Skills Development Fund Corporation)
**Eligible Program: Applied Engineering (AE)**

-   Loan rate between RM2,500 to RM24,000 depending on program/training phase
-   Allowance (RM400, RM600, RM800) + transport (one-off RM500 payment) are covered
-   Exclusively for Malaysia Skills Certificate (SKM), Diploma of Malaysia Skills (DKM), Higher Diploma of Malaysia Skills (DLKM) trainees

### Merit Scholarship
- Available for applicants with 6As and above in 2024 SPM
- Provides first semester course fee waiver worth RM4,000
- Exclusively for Diploma in Engineering and Diploma in Information Technology program students

### Industry Scholarship
- FutureTech Academy receives financial support from our Member Companies to assist students in their education

### Special Rebates
- First semester rebate of RM1,000 for Diploma in Engineering and Diploma in Information Technology program
