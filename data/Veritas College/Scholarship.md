# Scholarships & Financial Aid

At **BAC Education**, we believe that everyone should be able to *learn, play, work* and create in the digital economy.

That’s why, the **BAC Education Group** is delighted to offer you a variety of education funds and financial aid options to ease your burden whilst living up to our ethos of transforming lives through education. 

**#WeGotYourBACk**

### **The** **BAC** **Education Fund**

Our in-house education fund to grant financial scholarships up to RM 10 million to help you achieve your dreams:

*   **Merit-based Aid** for students with outstanding achievements, be it academic or extra-curricular activities.
*   **Financial Aid** for students with financial difficulties who require assistance to finance their education.

***Courses Available:***

The BAC Education Fund covers foundation, diploma, and degree courses in law, business, hospitality, communications, IT, accounting and finance, early childhood education, and culinary among others offered by BAC, Veritas University College, UNIMY, IACT College, and Reliance College.

***Eligibility:***

*   Malaysian citizens
*   International students

### **The** **BAC** **Sports Fund**

A competitive fund that rewards athletes who have competed either at state or international level in any sport.

***Courses Available:***

The BAC Sports Fund covers foundation, diploma, degree and postgraduate courses in law, business, hospitality, communications, IT, accounting, and finance among others offered by BAC, Veritas University College, UNIMY, IACT College and Reliance College

***Eligibility:***

*   Malaysian citizens competing at state or national level of any sport
*   International students competing at national level of any sport

[**Find Out More**](https://www.bac.edu.my/scholarships/?_gl=1%2A1ehqdzd%2A_gcl_au%2AMTU5NjgyMDE0MS4xNzYwMTg2NTIy)

### **PTPTN** **Loans**

PTPTN stands for Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (or National Higher Education Fund Corporation) and is a government institution that offers study loans specifically for tertiary education for Malaysian students.

***Courses Available:***

Any diploma, or degree course offered by the BAC Education Group.

***Eligibility:***

*   Malaysian citizens aged 45 years and below
*   An offer to study at an institution of higher learning
*   The remaining period of your study must not be less than one year

[**Find Out More**](https://www.bac.edu.my/scholarships/?_gl=1%2A1ehqdzd%2A_gcl_au%2AMTU5NjgyMDE0MS4xNzYwMTg2NTIy)

### **EPF** **Withdrawals**

This scheme allows EPF members to withdraw from their Account II for further studies in local or overseas institutions for their children or themselves.

***Courses Available:***

All diploma, degree and postgraduate programmes offered by the BAC Education Group.

***Eligibility:***

*   Malaysian citizens
*   Possess savings in Account 2
*   Studying at recognised local institutions or institutions abroad

[**Find Out More**](https://www.bac.edu.my/scholarships/?_gl=1%2A1ehqdzd%2A_gcl_au%2AMTU5NjgyMDE0MS4xNzYwMTg2NTIy)
