# Foundation

## certificate-communication-design


### URL

https://www.toa.edu.my/programmes/foundation/certificate-communication-design


### Programme Structure

**STUDY**

#### Semester 1 (14 Weeks)

- Basic Design
- Introduction to Digital Tools
- Basic Drawing 1
- Medium and Techniques
- Language Preparatory 1
- Malaysian Studies / Bahasa Melayu Komunikasi

#### Semester 2 (14 Weeks)

- Basic Drawing 2
- Visual Thinking
- Colour Fundamentals
- Art Appreciation
- Language Preparatory 2
- Bahasa Kebangsaan A
- Life Drawing Techniques

#### Semester 3 (14 Weeks)

- Basic Perspective Drawing
- Figure Fundamental Study
- Basic Letterforms
- Visual Composition & Layout
- Language Preparatory 3

#### Semester 4 (14 Weeks)

- Introduction to Visual Communication
- Figure Study
- Design Case Study
- Watercolour Drawing
- Integrity And Anti-Corruption

### Fee

result not found in online

### Duration

result not found in online

### Intakes

January, April, August

### Location

Bandar Sunway Campus, Penang Campus

### Entry Requirements

- Pass Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) with minimum 1 credit (1C) including a Pass in Bahasa Melayu and Sejarah
- Pass IGCSE / O-Level with minimum 1 credit (1C)
- Pearson BTEC Art & Design Level 2 Award/Certificate
- Pass UEC-SML and/or UEC technical with minimum Grade B in any one subject
- Pass Sijil Kemahiran Malaysia (SKM) Level 2 in the relevant aspect
