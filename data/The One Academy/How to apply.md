# ADMISSIONS

## HOW TO APPLY

Ready to kick-start your creative journey with one of the world's best art and design schools? It's easy to begin your professional education with everything you need listed right here.

[Let's get started](https://www.toa.edu.my/admission/application) on your 5-step application process!

### **1. Application Form**
[**Download**](https://www.toa.edu.my/admission/downloads/course_application_form.pdf) and fill out the application form with your particulars. You can submit it online, by mail or in person.

International applicants are advised to submit their application at least 8 weeks (2 months) prior to the commencement date of the intake to avoid any unnecessary delay in the admission process.

*If your application is accepted, you will receive our offer letter in 5 working days. If your application is not accepted, we will only refund your tuition deposit of RM1,500 / USD500.

### **2. Portfolio Sample**
Show us what youâ€™ve got! Submit a [sample](https://www.toa.edu.my/admission/domestic-student-guide#portfolio) of your portfolio:

- 1 Environment or Still Life drawing in colour, A4 size
- 1 Free Composition in any media, A4 size
AND
- 1 Portrait drawing (human) in pencil, A4 size
- 1 Fashion Design drawing in pencil, A4 size (applicable to Diploma in Fashion Design students)
- 1 Interior Design drawing in pencil, A4 size (applicable to Diploma in Interior Design / Foundation in Interior Architecture Design students)

### **3. Documentation Submission**
Please prepare to attach supporting documents, passport-sized photos, copies of your NRIC / Identity Card, academic transcripts and more. Refer to the [relevant guides](https://www.toa.edu.my/admission/domestic-student-guide) below for the complete list.

### **4. Registration Fee**
An application fee of RM2,000 / USD500 applies for each successful registration. If your application is not accepted, we will refund a partial amount of your tuition deposit (RM1,500 / USD375).

### **5. Placement Test**
Almost there! Take our English placement test and complete your application!

If you need any assistance, please contact our Admissions team at [info@toa.edu.my](mailto:info@toa.edu.my).

## DOMESTIC STUDENT GUIDE
Here you'll find a handy checklist of the essential requirements when submitting an application as a Malaysian student
### Application Checklist

- Documents & Items 
- Certified true copy of academic records (e.g. results, transcripts and certificates) 
- Certified true copy of school leaving / completion certificate

  2. Why do you want to enrol or pursue a career in this field of study? (150 words)

  - 1 Environment or Still Life drawing in colour, A4 size  
  - 1 Free Composition in any media, A4 size  
    AND  
  - 1 Portrait drawing (human) in pencil, A4 size  
  - 1 Fashion Design drawing in pencil, A4 size (applicable to Diploma in Fashion Design students)  
  - 1 Interior Design drawing in pencil, A4 size (applicable to Diploma in Interior Design / Foundation in Interior Architecture Design students) 
- Supporting documents for Credit Transfer (if applicable). Refer to [Course Application Form](https://www.toa.edu.my/admission/downloads/course_application_form.pdf) 
- Documentary evidence (if financed by scholarship/sponsorship/study loan) 
- Application Fees (RM2,000). Includes registration fee and tuition deposit (non-refundable and non-transferable) 
- Student Hostel Application Form (The hostel Booking Fee will be 1 month rental, Non-refundable)  
    (Only if required)

### Note

-   Please do not send cash for security reasons. The registration fee and tuition deposit must be received before your application will be processed.
-   If using telegraphic transfer/direct bank-in, you are reminded to attach the original payment transaction slip with the Application Form as proof of payment.
-   If your academic documents are in a language other than English/Bahasa Melayu, you must submit certified true copies of both the original documents and a literal translation.

### Additional documents if you are applying for BA (Hons) Level 4 (First Year) or Level 5 (Second Year)

-   For entry to Level 4, attach certified true copies of relevant Foundation qualifications and certificates
-   For entry to Level 5, attach certified true copies of relevant Diploma qualifications and certificates
-   Certified true copies of Course Syllabus/Subject Description
-   A folio of creative work to confirm that you have achieved the requisite learning outcomes (i.e. produce any artworks done in your previous recognised institution)

### Procedures of Obtaining A Student Pass

This process is explained in more detail in our Malaysian Immigration Section. In brief:

-   Student will need to submit all the documents to the application department of The One Academy.The documents that required will be as below:-
    -   A photocopy of all pages of your passport
    -   A copy of your photo (with white background)
    -   Payment for Student Visa
    -   Personal Bond deposit (According to the country)
    -   Health Declaration Form
    -   Academic results & transcripts
    -   Letter of eligibility (only for Iran Country)
    -   No Objection Certification (only for Sudan country).
-   Submit these documents to us. We will apply for your student pass prior to your entry. Once accepted, Immigration will release a Visa Approval Letter (E-VAL) and then you can make arrangements to enter Malaysia.
-   Application will take approximately 4-6 weeks.
-   Upon approval in Malaysia, you are required to do a medical checkup at our selected EMGS panel clinic and SSD staff will assist to bring the student for medical checkup.
-   Once you have done the medical check-up kindly submit your passport to the International Student Officer in order for immigration to do the student visa. This procedure takes approximately 2-6 weeks.
-   Our International Student Officer will inform you once the passport is ready for collection.

### Renewal of Student Visa

Your student pass / visa is only valid for 1 year. You are required to renew it annually throughout your course of study. (\*subject to change by authorities.)

### Procedure for Renewal of Student Visa

-   Student is required to submit soft copy of passport all pages and payment of RM 1,000.00 pay to the finance department 3 months before the student visa expiry date.
-   You are required to submit your passport to our International Students Department 1 month before the visa expiry date.
-   Student must ensure the CGPA should be more than 2.0 and attendance is above 80% otherwise the students visa application can be revoked by the authorities.
-   The renewal process will take approximately 1 to 2 months.
-   You will bear any overstaying penalty / compound.
-   Our International Student Officer will inform you once the passport is ready for collection.
