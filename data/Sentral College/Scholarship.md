    | **Diploma in Business Studies** |            2 years 4 months            |

    |     **Diploma in Computer Systems Technology**      |  2 years 4 months|
    |    **Diploma in Tourism Management**      |  2 years 4 months       |
    
-    The scholarship recipients must achieve and maintain a minimum level of academic performance (GPA 3.50 for every semester – result calculated does NOT include MPU courses) to continue to be eligible for the scholarship.
-   Applicants can hold ONLY one scholarship or rebate offered by SENTRAL at any one time
-   For more information, please contact 04-2621888

SENTRAL works closely with The Star newspaper to provide Scholarships to students to pursue programmes at SENTRAL. -   Diploma in Accounting
-   Diploma in Tourism Management
-   Diploma in Early Childhood Education
-   Certified Accounting Technician (CAT)
-   Association of Chartered Accountant (ACCA)

Eligibility:

-   Malaysian citizens not more than 25 years of age
-   Excel in extra-curricular activities or active in sports

-   Possess reasonably good results in STPM / UEC or equivalent (for Degree courses)

For further enquiries, please email: [edufund@thestar.com.my](mailto:edufund@thestar.com.my) or call 03-7967 1388 ext. 1642 / 1643

## Financial Aids / Loans

### Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (PTPTN Loan)

Applicable to students who are enrolled in the following programmes offered by SENTRAL:

-   Diploma in Business Studies
-   Diploma in Computer Systems Technology
-   Diploma in Accounting
-   Diploma in Tourism Management
-   Diploma in  Early Childhood Education
-   BSc (Hons) Business & Management
-   BSc (Hons) Accounting & Finance
-   BSc (Hons) Business IT

PTPTN provides education loans to students pursuing their diploma and degree in local institutions of higher learning. Applications must be made using the Education Loan Application Form which is available from PTPTN or SENTRAL College Penang. The completed application form must be submitted through SENTRAL College Penang within the specified date, i.e. twice every semester.

For additional information, contact SENTRAL College at 04-2621888 or visit PTPTN website at [www.ptptn.gov.my](https://www.ptptn.gov.my/) / hotline 03-21933000.

### Employees Provident Fund (EPF) Fund Withdrawal Scheme for Education

This scheme allows EPF members to withdraw from their Account II to pay fees for their children’s education or themselves at local or foreign institution. For more details visit KWSP website at [https://www.kwsp.gov.my/](https://www.kwsp.gov.my/en/)

### Penang State Education Loan

The Penang State Government Education Loan aims to provide education loans to Malaysian citizens who were born or receive part of their education in Penang state. For online applications/ more details, please visit the Penang state website at [epp.penang.gov.my](https://epp.penang.gov.my/)

### The Indian Scholarship & Trust Fund (ISTF)

The High Commission of India, Kuala Lumpur has been providing financial assistance under the ISTF to Malaysian students of Indian origin pursuing full-time diploma and degree programmes in institutions of higher learning in Malaysia. However, please note that the scholarship does NOT cover the full cost of studies and it is only a one-time financial assistance. For more information visit High Commission of India, Kuala Lumpur website.

### Indus Education Foundation

The Indus Education Fund was established to provide interest-free loans to Malaysian students from unprivileged communities to pursue pre-university, undergraduate and professional courses/studies (except medicine, dentistry and pilot training) at recognised Private Higher Educational Institutions (IPTS) in Malaysia. For more details, please visit: [http://indus.org.my/index.html](http://indus.org.my/index.html)

### Yayasan Kek Lok Si

Malaysian students applying to enter undergraduate programmes locally are eligible to apply for the Yayasan Kek Lok Si Financial Aid. For more details, please visit: [http://yayasankekloksi.org/](http://yayasankekloksi.org/)

### Penang Future Foundation Scholarship

Penang Future Foundation Scholarship funds youth 25 years old and below to undertake full-time undergraduate studies at public and private institutions (IPTA and IPTS) in Malaysia.

MQA accredited programmes in Science (exclude Medicine), Technology, Engineering, Mathematics (STEM), and Accountancy & Finance.

Priority will be given to Penangites or those who have studied in Penang and students who demonstrate excellent leadership, communication skills, teamwork and or other non-academic achievements.

For more details, please visit: [https://penangfuturefoundation.my](https://penangfuturefoundation.my/) or [info@penangfuturefoundation.my](mailto:info@penangfuturefoundation.my)
